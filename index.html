Welcome to Dr. Akanksha's Homepage

Currently I am serving as a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Mathematics and Mechanics at IIMAS-UNAM, Mexico. My research focuses on Elastic Wave Propagation and Applied Mathematics, with applications in areas such as subsurface imaging, seismic exploration, and the characterization of geological and engineered materials. If you share an interest in these research domains or would like to collaborate, don't hesitate to reach out. I would be delighted to connect with researchers and professionals working on similar ideas.

Let's explore the beauty of mathematics and its applications together.

Research Interests

As a researcher at IIT-ISM Dhanbad, my work focuses on theoretical seismology and elastodynamics. I aim to deepen the understanding of seismic wave propagation and the mechanical behavior of elastic materials under dynamic conditions. My research contributes to advancements in earthquake hazard assessment and the development of resilient infrastructure.
